The desired outcome of an internship is to make a student more employable, giving them the opportunity to demand more from an entry-level job and get ahead faster using skills and experience honed in a learning environment. Internships also allow students to gain experience in the working world, get comfortable with the job environment, and increase confidence in their abilities. Students who have interned in addition to classroom education stand out to employers as more qualified for hiring.
